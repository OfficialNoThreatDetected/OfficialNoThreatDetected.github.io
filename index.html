Description

No Threat Detected is a two player co-op puzzle game where the characters colour determines what they can interact with in the game. Science vessel Icarus flys to close to an asteroid field taking on significant damage when the only two repair bots (cr-46 and Shr1mp) go to fix it, the Icarus claims there’s no need, and that everything is just fine. As the two continue they find the asteroids field is home to an unknown creature.

History

No Threat Detected is a student project from Swinburne University, for many of us it being our final project. Our team decided to develop a co-op game as a celebration for people to be able to see each other after the lock-downs in 2020 and 2021. Our game progressed through semester 1 where we solidified our game concept and developed our alpha build. Development continued over semester 2 culminating in a build being showcased at PAX AUS 2022, where it was received well by players.

Features

  • Challenging puzzles, requiring teamwork to complete.
  • Beautiful pixel art, to create a retro feel to the game.
  • Compelling narrative, discover, what has been happening to the ship.
  • Customizable, experience, if narrative is not your thing or wanting to speed run the game, turn off dialogue so that gameplay never slows down.
